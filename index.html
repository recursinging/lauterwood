lauterwood

Manufacturing fine Eurorack cases since 2020

Lauterwood is: Sam Braam, an electronics and Eurorack enthusiast, and Robert Braam, a woodworking enthusiast.

We know that Eurorack module manufacturers, most of them small companies like our own, put countless hours of passionate effort into the design and function of their modules. We also appreciate the chaotic beauty of a patch, created over possibly months of experimentation, perhaps leading to a serendipitous musical discovery.

Our cases are not just to hold modules and patches, but to present them as the beautiful musical instruments they are.

frame:work

The frame:work series of 6U, 104HP Eurorack cases are designed to make your patch the centerpiece of your studio. The table-top format is ergonomic and stable, while the sweeping angles of the sides and rear make it stunning from any perspective.

Like a work of art, the frame:work case encompasses your modules and patches in a hand crafted frame.

Every frame:work case is unique. Have a look below at our currently available designs. Or perhaps a commisioned variation? Just contact us, we'll try to make it a reality.
