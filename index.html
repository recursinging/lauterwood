lauterwood

Building unique, hand-crafted, Eurorack cases since 2020

Lauterwood is the brain child of Sam, an electronics and Eurorack enthusiast, and Robert Braam, a recently retired woodworking enthusiast.

We know that Eurorack module manufacturers, most of them small companies like our own, put countless hours of passionate effort into the design and function of their modules. We also appreciate the chaotic beauty of a patch, perhaps created over months of experimentation, perhaps leading to a serendipitous musical discovery.

We create Eurorack cases not just to hold modules and patches, but to present them as a beautiful, heterogenous, musical instrument.
